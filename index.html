Pop By The Polls

This is a short research article for the 2020 eletion. It covers four issues currently being discussed, a candidate running for President of the United States, a polling place, and a sample ballot.

Issues

These are the issues that voters and candidates are discussing this election.

Coronavirus

Coronavirus continues to ravage the nation with over 200,000 dead.

Healthcare

Many feel that healthcare in America is too expensive.

Gun Control

High profile school shootings have made many question Americas love affair with firearms.

Climate Change

What can be done to stop global warming in its tracks?

Candidate

This is a person that has been nominated as a general election candidate for President of the United States.

Joe Biden

Joe Biden is the Democratic candidate for President of the United States with Kamala Harris as his running mate. Biden is running for the third time and this is his first time as a party nominee. As a candidate Biden supports a nationwide mask mandate, climate change reform, and expanding Obamacare while addng a public option. He's previously served as a US Senator from Delaware and as the Vice President to Barack Obama.

Polling Place

This is a location that voters can visit to cast a ballot for the candidates they prefer.

Optimist Park

Located in Northwest Raleigh near the intersection of Six Forks and Millbrook road, Optimist Park serves as the primary polling place in and around the Crabtree Valley area. Normally a gymnasium for youth basketball, the site serves as a polling place during elections.
